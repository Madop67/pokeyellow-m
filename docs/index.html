01 What it is

Everything about Yellow you remember, running on the battle engine you actually want.

Chromatic Yellow keeps Kanto's map, story, and 151 Pokémon, then modernizes the machinery underneath. Damage is decided by a full Gen 6+ type chart — with Steel, Dark, Fairy, Sound, and Light added as real attacking types — and each move has its own physical-or-special category instead of being locked to its type. Special Attack and Special Defense are separate stats, EVs and critical hits follow modern rules, and any Pokémon can Terastallize once per battle to change its type on the fly.

On top of the engine sits a complete content pass: the entire move list was redesigned so every type has a full damage ladder, and every trainer in the game — from the first bug catcher to the Champion — was rebuilt with a deliberate moveset and smarter AI. The guides below walk you through all of it.

03 Get the game

Chromatic Yellow ships as a patch you apply to your own Pokémon Yellow ROM.

Because this is a modification of a copyrighted game, only the patch is distributed — never a full ROM. You supply a legally-owned copy of Pokémon Yellow, apply the patch, and play the result in any Game Boy Color emulator or on hardware via flashcart.
